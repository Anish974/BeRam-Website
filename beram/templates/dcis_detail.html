What is DCIS?

BeRAM's Drone Cloud Intelligence System (DCIS) integrates cloud computing, AI, and 5G connectivity to manage drone fleets in real-time, enhancing safety and efficiency across industries.

Our DCIS represents the pinnacle of 6th generation AI-powered drone technology, enabling unprecedented capabilities in data processing, decision-making, and mission execution. By leveraging advanced cloud infrastructure and cutting-edge artificial intelligence, we've created a system that transforms how drones operate and deliver value.

Key Features

Real-Time Fleet Management

Utilize cloud-based infrastructure and 5G connectivity for seamless tracking, monitoring, and control of multiple drones, ensuring operational efficiency and safety.

AI-Driven Autonomy

Leverage advanced AI and machine learning for autonomous flight path planning, collision avoidance, and predictive maintenance, enhancing reliability and performance.

Cloud-Based Data Processing

Process and analyze massive amounts of data in real-time through our scalable cloud infrastructure, enabling instant insights and decision-making capabilities.

Enhanced Safety Protocols

Implement advanced safety features including geofencing, emergency response systems, and regulatory compliance tools to ensure secure operations.

Applications

Precision Agriculture

Monitor crop health, optimize irrigation, and apply targeted treatments with high-precision data collection and analysis, increasing yields while reducing resource usage.

Provide comprehensive monitoring of large areas, perimeter security, and rapid response capabilities for both public safety and private security applications.

Logistics & Delivery

Enable efficient last-mile delivery, medical supply transport, and logistics operations in hard-to-reach areas with autonomous drone fleets.

Environmental Monitoring

Track environmental changes, monitor wildlife, and assess natural resources with high-resolution data collection and AI-powered analysis.
