Applications

{% if solution.slug == 'agriculture' %}

Crop Monitoring

Regular aerial surveys to assess crop health, identify pest infestations, and monitor growth patterns throughout the growing season.

Precision Irrigation

Targeted Spraying

Apply fertilizers, pesticides, and herbicides precisely where needed, reducing chemical usage and environmental impact.

{% elif solution.slug == 'energy' %}

Regular monitoring of power lines to detect damage, vegetation encroachment, and potential failure points before they cause outages.

Detailed inspection of wind turbine blades and components to identify wear, damage, and maintenance needs without costly downtime.

Solar Farm Monitoring

Thermal imaging to identify underperforming solar panels and optimize energy production across large solar installations.

Site Surveying

Rapid and accurate topographic surveys and site mapping to support planning, design, and construction activities.

Progress Monitoring

Regular aerial documentation of construction progress to track timelines, verify work completion, and identify potential issues.

Volumetric Measurements

Accurate measurements of stockpiles, excavations, and material volumes to optimize resource management and billing.

Perimeter Security

Continuous monitoring of facility perimeters to detect unauthorized access and provide early warning of security breaches.

Event Security

Aerial surveillance of large events to monitor crowd movements, identify potential safety issues, and support security personnel.

Search Operations

Rapid deployment for search and rescue operations, covering large areas quickly with thermal and optical imaging capabilities.

{% elif solution.slug == 'logistics' %}

Delivery of essential supplies to areas with limited infrastructure or challenging terrain, such as islands, mountains, or disaster zones.
