Our Genesis: The Spark of Innovation
January 2024
BeRAM (Bhandarkar Excellence of Research and Management Pvt. Ltd.) began as an idea born out of curiosity, innovation, and the urge to solve complex societal challenges through deep-tech solutions. What started in a college lab at RCOEM, Nagpur, has today evolved into a multidisciplinary startup shaping India's drone and AI ecosystems.

Ideation to Incorporation
March 25, 2025
BeRAM Pvt. Ltd. was officially incorporated as a private limited company with a vision to solve real-world problems through research, student-led innovation, and scalable products — starting with autonomous drone delivery and surveillance.

Major Innovations & IPs
We proudly filed a patent for our revolutionary Power Harnessing System (PHS), enabling drones to recharge mid-air using the magnetic field of HV transmission lines. This IP reflects our commitment to engineering systems never imagined before.

Project Streams Under Development
BeRAM’s innovation ecosystem revolves around three core technologies: UTMS (Unmanned Traffic Management System) Real-time AI-driven drone airspace traffic management and disaster monitoring. PHS (Power Harnessing System) Mid-air charging for uninterrupted drone operations over transmission infrastructure. Drone Delivery MVP A scalable drone delivery system optimized for last-mile logistics in Indian terrains.
