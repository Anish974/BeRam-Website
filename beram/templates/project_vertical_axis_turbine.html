Project Overview

The Innovation

Vehicle-Induced Wind

Harnesses the wind generated by passing vehicles, a previously untapped energy source in urban environments

Dual-Purpose Design

Combines street lighting with energy generation, maximizing the utility of existing infrastructure

Sustainable Urban Planning

Creates a pathway to self-sustaining urban infrastructure with minimal environmental impact

Turbine Type Vertical Axis (Savonius-Darrieus hybrid)
Height 1.2 meters
Diameter 0.8 meters
Weight 45 kg
Power Output 100-400W (depending on traffic flow)
Cut-in Wind Speed 2.5 m/s
Battery Storage Optional 1.2 kWh lithium-ion
Design Life 15+ years
