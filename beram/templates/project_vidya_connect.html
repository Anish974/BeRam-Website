Project Overview

{{ project.long_desc | safe }}

Vidya Connect is designed to address the digital divide in education, particularly in rural and underserved areas of India where traditional internet infrastructure is limited or non-existent.

The Challenge

Limited Connectivity

Over 70% of rural India lacks reliable internet access, creating a significant barrier to quality education.

Infrastructure Gaps

Traditional internet infrastructure is costly and time-consuming to deploy in remote regions.

Content Filtering

Ensuring access to educational content while filtering out inappropriate material is a significant challenge.

Cost Barriers

Existing solutions are often too expensive for schools and educational institutions with limited budgets.
